Game Rules

Home | Play

1. Controls

  Keyboard (Recommended):

  • Change the ship direction using arrow keys
  • Press s or Left Click to shoot regular bullets
  • Press a or Right Click to shoot special bullets collected

  Touchscreen:

  • Swipe Up/Down/Left/Right to change the ship direction
  • Touch (one finger) to shot regular bullets
  • Multitouch (two fingers) to shoot special bullets collected

2. Bullets

  • Regular bullets:
    • Can be fired freely
    • Regular bullets have a 60% chance to destroy asteroids :))
    • Bounces back upon collision
    • Colliding with regular bullets will cause damage and loss of points
    • Obeys the laws of physics :))
  • Special bullets:
    • Limited quantity
    • Show current type in Ammo
    • Effects depend on the type of bullet
    • Has a 100% chance of destroying asteroids

3. Items

  • Burger: Heals
  • Money: Adds points
  • One bullet (shoot a red bullet): 100% destroys asteroids, bounces back when hitting walls, and heals upon hitting the player
  • Three bullets (shoot blue bullets): Bullets shot 100% destroy asteroids, do not bounce back when hitting walls
  • Swirl (circular bullet): Destroys all existing asteroids and bullets

4. Asteroids

  • Spawn from the central planet, quantity increases over time.
  • Collision with asteroids destroys them and causes damage to the player
  • Asteroids can break into smaller asteroids, damage and points depend on the size of the asteroid
  • Asteroids bounce back upon collision and follow the laws of physics :D
